About DocSendGo

DocSendGo is a file-sharing and document productivity platform designed for general users, students, offices, CSC centres, online service centres, xerox/print shops and businesses.

How it works

  1. A receiver opens their personal QR code.
  2. The sender scans that QR code.
  3. The sender uploads the file.
  4. The receiver can view, print or download it.
