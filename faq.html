Contact & Support

For support, report the issue with a screenshot, the affected page and a short description of what happened.

Before reporting a problem

  • Refresh the page and try again.
  • Check camera and popup permissions.
  • Confirm that the internet connection is working.
  • Do not include passwords, OTPs or private document contents in a public report.

Support contact

The permanent support email will be added before the app-store submission. Until then, use the official DocSendGo website communication channel.
