Frequently asked questions

Why does DocSendGo need camera access?

Camera access is used only when you choose to scan a QR code. You can deny the permission and use a code or another available method instead.

Which files can I send?

Use supported PDF and image files that you own or are authorised to share. File limits may vary by browser, device and storage configuration.

Why is a file not uploading?

Check the internet connection, file type and file size. Retry after reopening the app. Do not rename an unsupported file just to bypass the file-type check.

Can DocSendGo verify an Aadhaar, PAN or other document?

No. DocSendGo transfers or processes files; it does not provide official identity or document verification.

How do I receive a document?

Open Receive, enter the valid code or use your profile/QR workflow, and then view the available files. Do not share private receive codes publicly.

Can I use the app offline?

The native home and some cached pages may open, but sending, receiving and syncing documents require internet access.

How do I delete my data?

Use available delete controls. For records that cannot be deleted in the app, open the Contact page and submit a data deletion request with the relevant User ID or Share Code.

Where can I report a problem?

Use the official support website shown on the Contact page. Hide confidential information before sending any screenshot.
