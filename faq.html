Help & FAQ

How do I send a file?

Open Send, scan the receiver’s personal QR code, confirm the receiver and upload the file.

How do I receive a file?

Create or open your profile, show your personal QR code to the sender and check Received Files after upload.

Why does QR scanning not start?

Allow camera permission, use HTTPS and close any other app using the camera.

What can I do with a received file?

Supported documents can be viewed, printed or downloaded using the available buttons.

Does DocSendGo replace a backup service?

No. Keep an original copy of important files.
